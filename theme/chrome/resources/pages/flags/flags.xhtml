Careful, these flags may bite

WARNINGThese experimental features may change, break, or disappear at any time. We make absolutely no guarantees about what may happen if you turn one of these flags on, and your browser may even spontaneously combust. Jokes aside, this page controls some Silverfox functions, replacing the more manual way of going to about:config. Please proceed with caution.

Enable Profile Pictures

Custom and Custom - Animated are meant to be edited by user, expecting a .png or .gif respectively

Choose between the original profile pictures or your own seen in the top left corner. Mac or Linux users with reversed titlebar position will see them in top right corner.

Disables custom titlebars and Linux-specific System Theme customisations. Use this setting when combining Silverfox with a Windows-styled CSS modification.
