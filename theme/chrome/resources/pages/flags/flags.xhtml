Careful, these flags may bite

WARNINGThese experimental features may change, break, or disappear at any time. We make absolutely no guarantees about what may happen if you turn one of these flags on, and your browser may even spontaneously combust. Jokes aside, this page controls some Silverfox functions, replacing the more manual way of going to about:config. Please proceed with caution.

Flags

Be Chromium

Turns Chrome branding to Chromium branding, visible in multiple places such as homepage, icognito page, hamburger menu, settings page, favicons/bookmarks, etc.

Enable

Enable Aero Glass

For Windows 10 and Windows 11 ONLY. Requires Aero enabling software such as DWMBlurGlass or Glass8

Removes background of tabs bar for Native theme, allowing Aero to show itself. Custom Firefox themes will disable Aero to simulate Chrome's way of handling them.

Enable

Sorry, this flag is not available on your platform.

Restore Old Look

Turns new tab icon into older pre 2012 refresh design and hamburger menu turns into early 2012 wrench menu.

Enable

Allow Homepage Images

Allows custom user set images on homepage. Unfortunately Firefox themes don't natively support this. Silverfox optimized themes will also need this if they have a homepage image variable.

Enable

Enable Profile Pictures

Custom and Custom - Animated are meant to be edited by user, expecting a .png or .gif respectively

Choose between the original profile pictures or your own seen in the top left corner. Mac or Linux users with reversed titlebar position will see them in top right corner.

Enable Native Linux Titlebars

Linux only

Disables the custom Chrome styled titlebar buttons, allowing the native ones to show.

Enable

Sorry, this flag is not available on your platform.

Your changes will take effect the next time you relaunch Silverfox.
